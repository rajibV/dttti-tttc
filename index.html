Brand Details

Dr. Neamat Ulya Bhuiyan

Bangladesh is a small country with a large population of 160 million. This provides a huge potential for further economic development, but progress is hindered by three main factors: Poor infrastructure, Lack of quality training and the Lack of Skilled workforces. Lack of skills reflect in the countries high rate of unemployment and it also cause underemployment. Bangladesh has more than 10 million migrant workers, but most of them are low skilled, making money in low paid jobs. Bureau of Manpower, Employment & Training (BMET) operates 104 Technical Training Centre (TTC), 6 Institutes of Marine Technologies which have a focus on skills training. Moreover, TTCs will be established in every Upazila (subdistrict) in the near future.
